All Eyes one Me

Artis : Tupac

All Eyes One Me is a song by Tupac Shakur, released in 1997. It is a compilation album by the American rapper and producer Tupac Shakur, and is considered one of his most successful albums to date. The song was written by Shakur, and recorded by his band Rage Against the Machine. The song is often referred to as "the song that changed rap music".
